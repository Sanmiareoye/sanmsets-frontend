T & C's

Refills

I don't work on other peoples work, only my work. I don't refill sets more that 4 weeks old. Refills are charged the same as refular length sets.

Location

Tyrellstown Area Blanchardstown. Please request full address within 24 hours of your appointment.

Deposit

A deposit of €10 is required to secure your booking. Please ensure to check the confirmation email on how to send this.

Payment Options

We take payement through bank transfer and cash.

Late Fees

If you are up to 15 minutes late, a late fee of €10 will be added to your bill.

Out of Hours / Emergency Slots

If you need an appointment outside of the times available or hours 12pm - 6pm, an extra charge of €15 applies. Contact me at sanmsets@gmail.com to arrange this appointment.
